# Iconography

<br />

### **Introduction**

Icons are visual representations of commands, files, devices, directories, or common actions. <br />
They’re used to provide visual context and enhance usability. We rely on them as they help users find what they’re looking for quickly.

### **Sizes**

Icons scale by 4 pixels where the most common usage is 16x16px , 20x20px and 24x24px.

### **Library**

[Heroicons library](https://heroicons.com/) <br />
[Feather Icons library](https://feathericons.com/) for social icons

Our shadow system follows a principle of multiple of 2. <br />

### **Usage**

Shadows are used for creating user understanding and should simulate the natural, real-world behavior of light and shadow-casting. <br />
Shadows must be used consistently, to indicate possible interactions or reactions triggered by a user's action.
